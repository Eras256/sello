# SCF v7.0 Build Award - Budget Breakdown

## Total Request: $150,000 USD in XLM

This document outlines the allocation of funds across the four milestone tranches mandated by the Stellar Community Fund (SCF) v7.0 rules.

### Tranche 1: Activation (10% - $15,000)
*   **Goal:** Project startup, legal structuring, and initial tooling.
*   **Allocation:**
    *   Legal & Compliance (KYC/KYB setup, terms of service): $8,000
    *   Infrastructure (Cloud hosting, CI/CD pipeline setup): $3,000
    *   Design & Branding (UI/UX refinement): $4,000

### Tranche 2: Mid-development (20% - $30,000)
*   **Goal:** Completion of Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and core smart contracts.
*   **Allocation:**
    *   Smart Contract Development (AttestationStore, TierRegistry): $15,000
    *   Backend API Development (MPP integration, indexer): $10,000
    *   Frontend SDK Development (TypeScript client): $5,000

### Tranche 3: Testnet Readiness (30% - $45,000)
*   **Goal:** Full deployment on Stellar Testnet, comprehensive testing, and security audits.
*   **Allocation:**
    *   Security Audit (External firm for Soroban contracts): $20,000
    *   QA & Testing (E2E, integration, fuzzing): $10,000
    *   Frontend Integration (Web app connecting to testnet): $10,000
    *   Community Beta Program (Incentives for testnet users): $5,000

### Tranche 4: Mainnet Launch & UX (40% - $60,000)
*   **Goal:** Live mainnet deployment, UX verification, and ecosystem integration.
*   **Allocation:**
    *   Liquidity & Operations (Initial transaction fees, operational buffer): $20,000
    *   Marketing & Developer Relations (Hackathons, tutorials): $15,000
    *   Partner Integrations (Grants for protocols integrating SELLO): $15,000
    *   UX Audit & Refinements (Post-launch improvements): $10,000
