# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SELLO is part of the Stellar ecosystem. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be constructive**: Provide feedback that includes what went right or wrong, why, and what can be improved
- **Play to your strengths**: Focus your contributions where your expertise adds the most value
- **Be kind and supportive**: We're building a community together — a welcoming environment drives development
- **Be patient**: Especially with newcomers to the Stellar ecosystem
- **Be welcoming**: Actively seek diverse perspectives

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Violent threats or language directed at another person
- Discriminatory jokes and language (racism, sexism, ableism, etc.)
- Posting sexually explicit or violent material
- Posting (or threatening to post) other people's personally identifying information ("doxing")
- Personal insults, especially those using discriminatory terms
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behavior
- Repeated harassment of others — in general, if someone asks you to stop, then stop

## Stellar Community Alignment

This Code of Conduct aligns with and extends the official [Stellar Community Guidelines](https://stellar.gitbook.io/scf-handbook/governance/community-guidelines) and the [Stellar Development Foundation Code of Conduct](https://www.stellar.org/community/code-of-conduct).

As an SCF-funded project, all SELLO contributors and community members are expected to uphold the values of the broader Stellar ecosystem.

## Reporting

If you believe someone is violating this Code of Conduct:

1. **Stellar ecosystem issues**: Report to **community@stellar.org**
2. **SELLO-specific issues**: Open a private report via **conduct@sello.dev**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by the project leadership.

Participants asked to stop any harassing behavior are expected to comply immediately. Violations may result in:

1. **Warning**: A private written warning with clarity of violation
2. **Temporary ban**: Temporary exclusion from project spaces
3. **Permanent ban**: Permanent exclusion from project spaces

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all SELLO project spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Discord channels
- Social media accounts
- Events and meetups
- Any other forums managed by the SELLO team

Violations occurring outside these spaces may still affect a person's ability to participate within them.
